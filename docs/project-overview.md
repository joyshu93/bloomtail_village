# Project Overview

## Elevator Pitch
A warm, animal-resident village builder where the player shapes a small town full of cozy paths, tiny shops, and pleasant neighborhood spaces.

## Player Fantasy
- Make a town feel cute and lived-in.
- Keep residents happy by placing comfortable, natural, and useful spaces.
- Grow a charming village instead of optimizing a hard-edged economy.

## MVP Experience
- Start with a small blank map.
- Place roads, buildings, and decor.
- Watch residents move in, react, and ask for simple improvements.
- Read town feedback through a compact UI and a few friendly notifications.

## Non-Goals
- No electricity, water, transport, or production chains.
- No multiplayer.
- No deep simulation or large-scale city management.
