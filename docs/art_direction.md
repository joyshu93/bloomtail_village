# Art Direction Notes

## MVP look

- Top-down 2D presentation
- Placeholder shapes and color blocks are acceptable
- Soft grass-heavy palette with warm accents
- Avoid industrial or realistic visual language

## Recommended placeholder colors

- Grass: muted green
- Road: sandy beige
- Housing: peach / cream
- Commerce: warm coral / gold
- Public facility: dusty blue
- Decor: fresh green, flower pink, lamp amber

## UI tone

- Rounded panels with soft contrast
- Dense information is acceptable if grouped clearly
- Notifications should feel gentle, not alarmist
