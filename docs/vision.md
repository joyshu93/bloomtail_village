# Cozy Village Vision

## Core fantasy

The player is not building a giant efficient city. The goal is to create a tiny village that feels warm, lived-in, and welcoming to animal residents.

## Pillars

- Small scale over expansion pressure
- Readable systems over simulation depth
- Villager personality over anonymous population counts
- Pleasant atmosphere over harsh optimization

## MVP feel targets

- The map should look busier and cuter within the first three minutes.
- Every placement should tell the player what changed: score, happiness, or resident reaction.
- Roads matter because they make buildings feel connected, but they should stay easy to understand.
- Residents should create soft goals through requests rather than punishing failure states.
