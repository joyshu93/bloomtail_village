# Balance Spec

## Town Scores
Track four town metrics:
- Comfort
- Nature
- Convenience
- Atmosphere

## Suggested Early Effects
- Flowers: strong nature, small atmosphere
- Tree: nature, slight comfort
- Bench: comfort, small atmosphere
- Fence: comfort, minor visual structure
- Street lamp: atmosphere, small convenience
- Cafe: convenience, atmosphere
- General store: convenience
- Workshop: atmosphere or comfort depending on neighborhood context
- Plaza: broad positive effect with a mild central boost

## Happiness Logic
- Nearby decor should nudge happiness upward over time.
- Missing preferred environment should nudge happiness downward slowly.
- Effects should be small and readable, not dramatic.

## Economy
- The player should feel steady progress, not scarcity pressure.
- Early income should support experimentation with building layouts.
- Costs should encourage planning without causing frequent dead ends.
