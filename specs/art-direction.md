# Art Direction

## Tone
- Cozy, soft, bright, and inviting.
- Avoid harsh contrast and sterile city-builder visuals.

## Visual Language
- Simple top-down shapes are acceptable for MVP.
- Use friendly colors and readable silhouettes.
- The map should feel like a miniature toy town.

## UI Feel
- Compact, warm, and easy to scan.
- The main screen should do most of the work.
- Secondary popups should support, not dominate, the playfield.

## Placeholder Strategy
- Use solid colors, simple icons, and basic tiles if needed.
- Make sure functionality is clear before investing in polish.
